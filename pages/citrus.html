How to grow Sweet lemon (Citrus limetta) plant from seeds.

                            
                            citrus limetta plantlet
							
    Common name: Sweet lemon

    Botanical name: (Citrus limetta)

    Family name:  Rutaceae 

        How to grow the Sweet lemon plant at home?  

               * Growing sweet lemon plant is a very easy methods.  

               * We can easily grow it in our home if we follow these methods.

        Preparation of seeds: 

               * The first step is to select whole seeds that will not cause any damage.

               * The size of the selected seeds should also be correct.

                             
                                 Seeds 

               First the seeds should be soaked in a distilled water for 24 hours.

                             
                                 Soaking the seeds  
    
               After 24 hours the outer layer on top of the seeds should be removed.
			   
                             
                                 Easy to split  
       
	    Main things
            Bowl ,

            Cotton , 

            Coconut husk.

                             
                                 Main things  
 
               In a bowl mix the coconut husk with the water.

                             
                                 Coconut husk  
                                                      
               Then fold the seeds with cotton and soak them in water in a bowl.

                             
                                 Cotton with seeds  
                                           

               Soaked seeds with cotton are closing in box without any air circulation.          

                             
                                 Box  
                                                                 
               Seeds are prepared.
 
                             
                                 Germinated seeds  
                                                                                   
               After 1 week the seeds are ready to germinated.

        Preparation of Plant:

            Sow the germinated seeds in the soil.

            Keep soil wet.

                             
                                 Plants ready  


        After 7 days  we shall see the plant growth.
 
        This steps is follow we can get the plant growth of sweet lemon plant.

        Let's grow tree! Let's benefit! Let's get rain!
